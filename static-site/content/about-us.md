# About Us

<br>

## Our Mission

We are an NFPO dedicated to the success of the Rust Programming Language and its users in Japan.

## Who We Are

We are an incorporated not-for-profit organization based in Tokyo. We accept donations, and offer in return our gratitude and dedication to the Rust community. Thank you for your support!

## How We Use Your Donations

In summary, our operating expenses include:

- Paying for computing services such as Google Workspace, Cloudflare, etc.
- Renting venues for in-person events.
- Buying food and drinks for in-person events.
- Buying or renting equipment to host and record talks.

In detail, our efforts include, but are not limited to, the following:

- Build and maintain an online presence for the Tokyo Rust community, giving visibility to our members and projects.
- Organize in-person events where community members can meet and share their knowledge and experience of building software with Rust.
- Operate an online messaging system where members can share resources and discuss topics related to building software with Rust.
- Maintain and facilitate projects that contribute to open-source software.
- Organize events where members can collaborate towards a common goal or theme.
